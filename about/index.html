About

Who am I?

  • SDR

WTF is going on here?

electro.pizza is a smiple place to throw notes and updates on the things I’m working on. As I progress in a project I will often forget some of the challenges faced along the way. Hopefully noting details and process as I am working will help keep them from slipping from memory.

Wanna talk about it?

There are no real plans to add a comment section to this site. However, if you have any questions or just want to give me a shout, fritter, rotonde, twitter, or Freenode IRC (rho) are good ways to do that.
